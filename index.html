What Are We About?

Our mission is to foster a community of passionate students in product at UCI. We host speaker events, workshops, and a two-quarter fellowship through our student-run product incubator.

What's In Store?

  • Speaker Events

    Speaker events and workshops with a variety of product professionals from top companies and startups.

  • Product Incubator

    An application based fellowship where passionate students are placed into teams and spend 1 to 2 quarters building a product from ideation to launch, while being taught by experienced student leads.

  • Career Mentorship

    Workshops sessions and mentorship opportunities to advance your career including internship advice, resume reviews, mock interviews, networking events, and more.
